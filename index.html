About Us

Greenland Bricks & Polls Ltd is a family-owned business specializing in manufacturing quality bricks and producing sturdy fence poles. With years of experience, we serve builders, contractors, and homeowners with dependable products and timely delivery.

  • Local manufacturing for consistent supply
  • Custom orders and color options
  • Delivery and site support for large builds
